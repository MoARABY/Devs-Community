student activity created in 2022 to empower tech students by helping them level up their programming skills, work on real projects, and get ready for the job market. Our work is divided into several parts including Programming, HR, Lecturers, and Media.

What We Do

We organize wide programming seminars and intensive bootcamps to explore different fields of programming, where both HRs and instructors participate voluntarily under real-work standards We also run professional media workshops executed as full campaigns just like in real companies, in addition to hosting large competitive contests that enhance the essential soft skills required in the job market.
